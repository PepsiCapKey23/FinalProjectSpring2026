## What idea(s) do you have for your final project?

An idea for this final project that I want do to is a game I used to play with my cousins in Ecuador. The game is pretty simple:
 - Draw a circle (or more inside) with chalk or make a circle in the dirt depending where you are playing
 - Inside you put in certain amount of marbles depending on the amount of players(can be 2-6 players, but for now lets do 2)
 - You begin from the starting line and flick your marble to knock out a marble from inside the circle(s)
 - If you hit a marble out, you now get that marble! Go again if you hit one out
 - If you miss, next player goes!
 - Get the most marbles out!
